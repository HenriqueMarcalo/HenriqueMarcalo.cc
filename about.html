How did it all start?

As someone who was born as a member of Gen Z, growing up in a world increasingly driven by technology I was amazed by the endless possibilities it offered. My parents always wanted to equip me with knowledge and immerse me in technology, recognizing its rapid evolution. They wanted to make sure I had fun in different ways they could have never experienced when they were my age, and at the same time ensured that I was well prepared for the ever-changing landscape ahead. Realizing that, I embraced it and from there on out the only thing I ever wanted to do was being somehow tied to technology.

Why Computer Science?

I had made my decision of having a career in technology, but what I didn't know was what I wanted to do specifically. There is a plethora of courses out there, so why did I end up choosing Computer Science? I found myself drawn to this course for its unparalleled versatility, because the way I see it, CS is a gateway to a multitude of career paths. I have a keen interest in the several pathways available in this field of work, so it was a clear-cut decision.

My key interests

Cybersecurity

Cyber threats have become more common in the past few years, and will only increase in frequency as technology advances and our reliance on digital systems grows. Cybersecurity professionals are in high demand and will continue to be for a long time, so if I have an opportunity to pursue this as a career I would be thrilled to put my skills into work to ensure online safety.

Video game Development

I can say with confidence that video games are a part of me, and that it's a different reality making one yourself with several challenges along the way. I'm currently embarking on a journey to create a small game with Unreal Engine, so it's already a step in the right direction. If my current project is a success, I would gladly want it to be the first of many adventures in video game development.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

This is probably the one with most potential. AI offers advances in technology we could possibly never imagine, and it is very much understandable. The fact that you can shape entire industries with AI tells me that much. Progress and innovation sits on top of everything, and currently the peak of that iceberg is Artificial Intelligence. I have high expectations for this field of work and its future.
